Welcome to jBash

jBash is a JavaScript "website" for simulating a linux style console in the browser. There are some built in commands like "load" and "dir" and you can easily attach new commands.

Instead of running apps, you can load pages directly into the console. (These pages are located in the index-directory of your server.)
